Description

Tasbih Pak Jamal was developed to assist an elderly person doing a religious activity. He used free tasbih app in the past but he decided to stop using it after an unfortunate incident happened. When he was doing dzikir, a pornographic ad shows and he accidentally clicked it which bring him to embarrassment.

He knew about ads but never bother with it because it didn't cause a lot of issues, but since that incident, he decided to put an end. After listening and studying how the app works, he asked me to develop an app that can work just like the previous app, which is for tasbih only.
