Description

Some clients requested an app version of their e-portfolio. Since I already adjusted the portfolio to fit the screen size, I only need to work on the apk. Although, some clients need major change due to additional requests and compatibility.

At first, I used PhoneGap because it was easy and familiar. But, from time to time, I occasionally change to MIT App Inventor because it is easier and some of my clients can actually use this application. At some point, clients requested to prepare their e-portfolio app using MIT App Inventor.
